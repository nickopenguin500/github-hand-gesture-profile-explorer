How to Use Gestures

🟢 Click & Select

Pinch your index finger and thumb together with one hand to click buttons or select repositories. The cursor will turn green.

🟠 Pan & Scroll

Hold your hand completely open and flat (all fingers extended). The cursor will turn into an orange radar. Move your hand to scroll the page.

🔵 Zoom In & Out

Pinch with both hands simultaneously. While holding the pinch, move your hands apart to zoom in, or closer together to zoom out.

📸 Scan Username

Pinch the "Scan Username" button. Hold a piece of paper with a valid username written clearly in dark marker up to the camera.
